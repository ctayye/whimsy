Biography


Kanye Omari West, born June 8, 1977, is an American rapper, record producer, and fashion designer from Chicago, Illinois. He is one of the most critically acclaimed musicians of the 21st century, widely regarded as one of the greatest artists of all time.

"I am not a rapper. I am an idea that cannot be contained."
